Biography

Shanshan XU is currently working toward the Ph.D. degree in Physical Geography at the School of Geographic Sciences, East China Normal University. She is currently focusing on research including inversion of remotely sensed imagery, evolution of air-water vapor contours, and hydrological modeling under the supervision of Prof. Hongming He. As the first author, she has published many papers in high-level SCI journals and international conferences in remote sensing and hydrology, such as IEEE Journal of Selected Topics in Applied Earth Observations and Remote Sensing (JSTARS), Sustainability, and ICCSMT.
